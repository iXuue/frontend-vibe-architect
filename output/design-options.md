# Output: Design Options

## Use When

Use after requirement summary, track choice, and initial mode selection.

```markdown
- Recommended option:

```

## Do Not Omit

- Do not omit `Not good for`.
- Do not omit risks.
- Do not omit why the option fits the requirement.
- Do not produce only one option for broad, high-impact, or visually ambiguous work.
- Do not treat reference examples as mandatory unless confirmed.
